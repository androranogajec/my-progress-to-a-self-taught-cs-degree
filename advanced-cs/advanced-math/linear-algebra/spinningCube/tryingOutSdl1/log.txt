Cube vertices have to be from (-1 -1 -1) to (1 1 1) i.e 0.5 of a cube shall occupy each quadrant, 
the center would be (0 0 0), in the case of (0 0 0) to (1 1 1), the center is going to be at (0.5 0.5 0.5)
